About

I am a final year Ph.D. student at IIT Hyderabad, advised by Prof. Vineeth N Balasubramanian. My research focuses on trustworthy machine learning and on multimodality, with recent work on enhancing visual reasoning in Multimodal Language Models. You can find my CV here.

Research Interests

  • Visual reasoning in Multimodal LLMs (spatial reasoning, grounding, RLVR/RL post-training)

  • Human-in-the-loop / preference-aware learning (algorithmic recourse)

  • Robustness, interpretability, and reliability of ML systems
